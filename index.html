Steps to make a sandwich

  1. Get all the vegetables you want to add in your sandwich.
  2. Take 2 slices of bread.
  3. Take mayonnaise of you choice (I prefer thousand island flavour) in a bowl and add all vegetables in the bowl.
  4. Mix all the ingredients properly.
  5. Put the mixture on a bread slice.
  6. Add a cheese slice and cover with another bread slice.
  7. Grill the sandwich in butter.
  8. Enjoy your sandwich!
